About GY'Oreal

Our Background

A story of
passion and elegance

GY'Oreal was founded on a single premise: that premium makeup should never come at the cost of comfort or conscience. Our founders, inspired by decades of high fashion and a love for inclusive beauty, set out to create a brand that honoured every skin tone and every story.

From our first lipstick to our award-winning foundations, each formulation is the result of rigorous testing, thoughtful ingredient sourcing, and an unwavering commitment to the artistry of beauty.

Today, GY'Oreal stands as a modern icon — a brand that seamlessly bridges luxury aesthetics with everyday accessibility. We believe beauty is not a standard to meet; it is an expression to explore.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Three core principles guide every decision we make — from ingredient sourcing to campaign creation.

Uncompromising Quality

Every ingredient is carefully selected. Every formula is tested across diverse skin types. We accept nothing less than exceptional performance.

Radical Inclusion

Beauty has no single face. Our shade ranges, our campaigns, and our community celebrate every skin tone, texture, and identity.

Sustainable Beauty

We are committed to reducing our environmental footprint — from recyclable packaging to responsible sourcing across our entire supply chain.

Looking Forward

“To redefine beauty as a space of freedom, creativity, and belonging for all.”

Our vision is a world where everyone can find themselves in the products they reach for — where makeup is not a mask, but a mirror.
